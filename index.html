Enter a number!

  • When you type in a number and press enter, a list of numbers will show up. Counting up to whatever number you enter! Some rules will be applied to this list:

  • Numbers with zero in them will be replaced with "Beep!".
  • Numbers with one in them will be replaces with "Boop!".
  • Three is not an agreeable number.


  • Please only enter whole numbers.
